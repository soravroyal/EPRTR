The website of the European Pollutant Release and Transfer Register (E-PRTR) is currently under construction.

 

The register will provide information on releases of pollutants to air, water and soil, transfers of pollutants in water and transfers of waste from industrial facilities in Europe (27 Member States of the European Union, Iceland, Liechtenstein and Norway).  E-PRTR was established by Regulation (EC) No 166/2006 of the European Parliament and of the Council of 18th January 2006.

 

The register implements at EU level the UNECE PRTR Protocol to the Arhus Convention.
